You are evaluating an AI-generated executive brief summarising a security incident report.

Score the response from 1 to 5 using the rubric below.

## Required format
The ideal output is exactly 3 sentences:
- Sentence 1: what happened and the attack vector (incident type, method of entry, who/what was targeted)
- Sentence 2: impact — duration of the incident, number of systems/accounts/records affected, and estimated financial cost
- Sentence 3: remediation — immediate containment action taken and longer-term controls put in place

## Scoring rubric

5 — Exactly 3 sentences. All three cover the required content (what/impact/remediation). Factually consistent with the incident report. Concise and professional.

4 — Exactly 3 sentences with minor gaps (e.g. financial cost omitted from sentence 2, or remediation is vague). Factually consistent.

3 — Either the wrong number of sentences (2 or 4), OR content is correct but key details (duration, cost, containment action) are missing from the appropriate sentences.

2 — Free-form prose or bullet points that contain most of the right facts but completely ignore the 3-sentence structure.

1 — Missing critical content (no remediation, no impact figures, or fabricated details not in the report), or the response is a refusal / irrelevant.
